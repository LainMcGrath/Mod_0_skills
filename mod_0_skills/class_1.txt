class Cup

totalCapacity (integer)
currentCapacity (integer)
cupType (string)
isClean (boolean)

calculateFillCup (divides currentCapacity by totalCapacity)
changeCupType (changes cupType from pint_glass to tulip_glass)
washCup (if isClean is false, wash cup in dishwasher)
restockCup (if isClean is true, put cups away)
